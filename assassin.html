Shadow Step: (Type: Verbal Range: self School: Sorcery, Uses: 2/life)

“I Step into the Shadows” x1

Effect: E: Player becomes Insubstantial.

Assassinate: (Type: Verbal Range: 20' School: Death, Uses: Unlimited)

Incantation: Say the word “Assassinate” immediately upon killing a person.

E: The victim is Cursed.

Material: Red strip

Blink: (Type: Verbal Range: self School: Sorcery, Uses: 2/life)

“I Step into the Shadows” x1

Effect: E: Player becomes Insubstantial and can move to any location within 50’ from their starting point..

Limitation: Caster may not end State within 10’ of a living enemy.

Hold Person: (Type: Verbal Range: 20' School: Command, Uses: 1/life)

“I command thee to stop” x3

Effect: Target player becomes Stopped for 30 seconds.

Teleport: (Type: Verbal Range: self School: Sorcery, Uses: 2/life)

“I travel through the aether” x5

Player becomes Insubstantial and moves directly to a location chosen at the time of casting by the caster. Upon arrival, they must immediately end the effect as per Insubstantial.

If the player’s Insubstantial state is removed before they have reached their destination, the effects of Teleport end.

Coup De Grace: (Type: Verbal Range: 20' School: Death, Uses: 1/life)

Incantation: “Death shall come for thee” x3

E: Target player dies.

Limitation: Target must be Wounded when the caster begins the Incantation. Although it still functions even if they are healed by the end of the Incantation.
