Let the white light of healing mend thy bones.
Let the white light of healing close thy wounds.
Let the white light of healing restore thy vigor.
The white light of healing hath healed thee.”

Effect: Heal a Wound

Release: (Type: Verbal, Range: Touch, School: Sorcery, Uses: 1/life Charge X3)

Incantation: “From thy bindings thou art released” x5

Effect: A single ongoing effect or State is removed from the target. Casters choice.

Limitation: Cannot remove Cursed.

Shadow Step: (Type: Verbal Range: self School: Sorcery, Uses: 1/life)

“I Step into the Shadows” x1

Effect: E: Player becomes Insubstantial.

Dispel Magic: (Type: Verbal, Range: 20', School: Sorcery, Uses: 1/ref)

Incantation: “By my power I dispel that magic” x3

Effect: All Enchantments on target are removed

Will work through Protection from Magic, Enlightened Soul, and similar magics and abilities. Will work on players that are Frozen or Insubstantial.

Evolution: (Type: Trait, School: Sorcery)

Effect: May wear an additional Enchantment. Evolution does not count towards the bearer’s Enchantment limit

N: This ability does work in conjunction with Attuned or Essence Graft so long as the other limitations of those Enchantments are followed.

Hold Person: (Type: Verbal Range: 20' School: Command, Uses: 1/life)

“I command thee to stop” x3

Effect: Target player becomes Stopped for 30 seconds.

Adaptive Blessing: (Type: Enchantment, Range: Self, School: Protection, Uses: 1/life)

Material: White Strip

Incantation: “I enchant thee with this blessing” x3

Effect: Bearer becomes Resistant to one of the following Schools: Death, Flame, Subdual, Command, Sorcery. School is chosen at the time of casting. Does not count towards a players Enchantment limit, may not be worn with any other Enchantments from the Protection School.
